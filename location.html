Location

Broerstraat 5

9712 CP Groningen

The spatial lecture halls in Academy Building are the perfect place to attend the Marathon. The convenience o the location will facilitate arrival, the building being in the center, a walk away or a bike away. If you do take the bike, we must let you know in advance, it can be quite challenging to find parking in front of the building, so we would advise to leave your bike somewhere close and walk the rest of the way while enjoying the buildings.

Once you arrive take the lift or the stairs to the last floor where you will find Offerhauzaal.
